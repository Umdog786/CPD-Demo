Various LinkedIn Learning Courses

LinkedIn Learning is a platform hosted by LinkedIn which allows people to take part in a variety of courses and earn certifications for that specific course. I used it to expand my knowledge around various computer science areas that I was interested in. The courses themselves were a combination of expert-taught lectures as well as quizes at the end of each chapter in order to test understanding of the topic. In order to achieve the certification, you had to watch all the lectures and complete/pass all the chapter quizes. In total I have completed 4 courses in their entirety, collecting 7 hours of CPD, however I will be completing a 5th in the near future to reach the 10 hour per-activity CPD hour cap. The four courses I completed are listed below and the certificates can be viewed at the bottom of this page. The certificates can be clicked to see a larger version.

  • Machine Learning with Sickit-Learn: 1 Hour
  • Web Development Foundations - Web Technologies: 2 Hours
  • Introduction to Data Science: 2 Hours
  • Database Foundations - Core Concepts: 2 Hours
