Various MLH Learn Day Events

Learn Day is a global day-long conference, sponsered by Microsoft that allows students from around the world to pick up new skills and learn about new areas of Computer Science that they might not have been taught before. I found the experience very fufilling as it allowed me to learn about topics I didn't really know anything about, such as Blockchain and Cryptocurrency, as well as teaching me key skills such as how to perform in a Technical interview and how to automate tasks to make coding easier. Around this page you will find some of the posters for the various MLH events as well as some evidence of my confirmed registration to those events. There were a number of events taking place along the week and I decided to sign up to 6 of the events available. The 6 I signed up for were:

  • MLH Local Hack Day - Introduction to MLH and all the events
  • MLH Blockchain - Introduction to Blockchain and Cryptocurrency
  • MLH Full Stack - Introduction to Full Stack Development
  • MLH Power Automate - Learning how to automate everyday tasks
  • MLG Technical Interview - Practicing computing Job Interviews
